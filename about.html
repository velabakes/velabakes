Our Journey

Our Story & Legacy

25 Years of Dedication and Craftsmanship

Crafting Delicious Memories

Since 2000, Vela Bakes has been crafting delicious memories with passion, quality, and tradition. For 25 years, we've been dedicated to bringing fresh, flavorful baked delights to our customers, using trusted recipes and the finest ingredients. What started as a humble bakery has grown into a beloved name, thanks to the support of our loyal customers. As we celebrate our 25-year legacy, we remain committed to baking every product with the same care, taste, and excellence that has defined us since day one.

Chronology of Trust

The Legacy Timeline

2000

A Humble Beginning

Started in Karumandisellipalayam, Perundurai with just one truck, three dedicated labourers, and a commitment to fresh baking.

2014

A Major Setback

Faced a sudden major business setback that tested our resolve, resulting in loss of operations. We chose to stand strong.

01.04.2014

Restarting from Zero

With renewed energy, absolute faith, and absolute effort, we restarted operations from the ground up on April 1st, 2014.

Present Day

Expanding Horizons

Now operating 8 trucks and managing a team of 50+ labourers. Our operations scale covers most of the hill regions of Tamil Nadu.

Our Impact

Pioneering Perundurai

Because of this legacy, Perundurai has become widely famous for bakery distribution, with over 10 similar bakery companies modeling themselves after our structure.
